# Chapter 2

As Anna walked through the park, she imagined the wonderful performances she could give with such a wand. “If I find it, I’ll be the greatest magician in all of New Orleans!” she said out loud, her eyes sparkling with excitement. Just then, Leo hopped beside her, his green skin glistening in the sunlight.

“Did I hear something about magic?” Leo asked with a cheeky grin, his big eyes twinkling. “Because if we’re talking about magic, you need my help!”

Anna laughed. “Of course, Leo! You have a knack for finding things. But how can you help me find the wand?”

“Simple!” Leo croaked confidently. “I know the park better than anyone. We just have to follow my frog instincts and maybe hop into some misadventures!”

Anna clapped her hands. “Let’s do it! Where do we start?”
